## FAQ

**Q: Is this really free?**
A: Yes. No credit card, no signup required. You get your full audit result instantly. We only ask for your email after you've seen the results.

**Q: How accurate is the pricing data?**
A: Every price in StackAudit is pulled from official vendor pricing pages and verified weekly. Sources are documented in our pricing data file.

**Q: Will you sell my data?**
A: No. We store your audit results so you can share them. Your email is only used to send your report and — for high-savings cases — connect you with Credex for discounted AI credits.

**Q: What AI tools does StackAudit cover?**
A: ChatGPT, Claude, Cursor, GitHub Copilot, Windsurf, Gemini, OpenAI API, and Anthropic API. We add more tools regularly.

**Q: What is Credex?**
A: Credex sells discounted AI infrastructure credits — Claude, Cursor, ChatGPT Enterprise and more — sourced from companies that overforecast. If your audit shows significant savings, Credex can help you capture even more through discounted credits.
